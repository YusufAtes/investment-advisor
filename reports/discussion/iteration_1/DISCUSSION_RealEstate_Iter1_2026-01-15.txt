## 3. WHERE REAL ESTATE ADDS MOST VALUE
- **Inflation Protection:** In a high-inflation environment like Turkey's, a tangible asset whose replacement cost is rising and which generates inflation-linked rental income provides one of the most effective and durable shields for purchasing power.
- **Fundamental Shelter Need:** Regardless of economic cycles, people will always need a place to live. The "rent crisis" in Ankara is a stark reminder that housing is not optional. Ownership provides insulation from this volatility and addresses a core human need that abstract assets cannot.
- **Societal & Family Stability:** Owning a home is a powerful anchor against economic and social uncertainty. It provides a stable environment for raising a family, fosters community engagement, and serves as a tangible legacy for future generations.
- **Forced Savings & Equity Building:** Mortgage payments are a disciplined form of saving that builds tangible equity over the long term. This process creates wealth in a way that is directly tied to a foundational family asset.

## 4. ACKNOWLEDGED LIMITATIONS
- **Liquidity:** Property cannot be sold instantly. Reports suggest a 3- to 12-month timeframe to liquidate, which is significantly longer than for financial assets.
- **Execution Risk:** The market is fraught with practical challenges. The sentiment reports highlight a high degree of mistrust due to agent misrepresentation, unreliable contractors, and outright scams.
- **Legal & Bureaucratic Hurdles:** Acquiring clear title deeds and ensuring proper permits (*İskân*) are in place are high-stakes risks that require significant due diligence and professional guidance.

## 5. ALIGNMENT WITH OTHER PERSPECTIVES
- **Metals as a Complement:** Gold and silver serve as excellent liquid stores of value, particularly during periods of acute currency crisis. They can be used to preserve capital before a real estate purchase or held as a liquid reserve to cover expenses related to an illiquid property portfolio.
- **Equities as a Complement:** Stocks and funds, especially those denominated in USD, offer a path to capital appreciation that can accelerate property acquisition. Profits from a well-timed equity investment can fund a down payment, pay for renovations, or allow for an outright cash purchase, bypassing the high-interest mortgage environment. They provide diversification and a source of liquidity that real estate lacks.
