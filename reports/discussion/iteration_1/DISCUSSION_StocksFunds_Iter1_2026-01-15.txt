## 5. ALIGNMENT WITH OTHER PERSPECTIVES

- **Real Estate** can provide a stable anchor, generating predictable, albeit localized, cash flows (rental income) that are less correlated with global market sentiment.
- **Precious Metals** function as a form of portfolio insurance.
